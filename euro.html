European

Raùl González Blanco

He is considered one of the best Spanish players of all time. He played for Real Madrid and was named the captain from 2003 and 2010 when he left. He has the record of scoring the most goals for this formidable team: 323 goals. He assisted Real obtain six league titles and three UEFA Champions League crowns. In Germany, while playing for FC Schalke, he was recognized for scoring the most goals in the Champions League. Sadly for him, Messi and Ronaldo have surpassed his achievement.

Roberto Baggio

Baggio is a world class player who played for Italy in the 90's. He was a decisive factor in the World Cups he played in. Even though he was a positive influence he is widely renouned for missing the decisive penalty kick against Brazil. He plays as a striker or attacking midefield. He has played for many powerful teams such as Fiorentia and Juventus.

Xavi Hernández Creus

He is one of the best midifielders around and some people think he may be one of the best Spanish players. He announced his retirement from international play after the 2014 World Cup. Xavi has played with Barcelona since he was 11. He has pin-point accuracy and his assists and goals have led Barcelona and Spain, to victory.
